an album about loving yourself because we kind all need to do that.

ily (in all lowercase, pronounced 'I. L. Y.') is my debut studio album released October 22, 2021.

The majority of the album was made over the course of a single week during spring break in my senior year of high school. I held onto the album and tweaked it a couple of times in order to have it release in my first semester of college.

Track-by-track

  • Maybe I Am Okay is the opening track that takes a while to get going. It's a slow introduction into the album and has a massive payoff at the end.
  • Smoke Up is about peer pressure getting to you and wanting a means to escape it. Also has a bonus surprise in the middle.
  • Fell Apart is a song about reflection, about wanting change. One of the very short songs to break up the tracklist.
  • Carly is what one of my friends called me for the longest time because to them it was the genderbent version of my name. Also has a fun synth solo at the end.
  • I don't want you here is about not feeling happy with yourself and desperately wanting to be someone else and also the centerpiece of the album.
  • wait nvm is what I told myself when it was all over.
  • New Shorts is about getting a new pair of clothes. Finding happiness in the little things in life and knowing there's brighter days ahead.
  • You're Beautiful To Me is what I told myself when I realised my true self worth. A purely happy song that belongs towards the end of the album.
  • I Love You is not a title track, but the longest one on here including a medley of previous songs. A perfect ending.
