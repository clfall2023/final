an EP perfect for festive season at night

Christmas-Chan is an EP released November 25, 2022.

This EP was made in a time where I only listened to lo-fi hip hop, and was really in a festive mood. Because of this, I wanted to make a fun side project while I work on my second album.

Tracklist

  1. snowfall (2:23)
  2. carol of the bells (3:24)
  3. golden leafless trees (2:38)
  4. jingle bell rock (4:15)
  5. christmas night (2:29)

Track-by-track

  • snowfall is an original track intended to be listened to as snow falls, as the title implies.
  • carol of the bells is one of my favorite Christmas songs and it's an ambitious remake of the classic song, with tiny twists and turns.
  • golden leafless trees is a remake of an old original that is remastered and notably shorter than the orignal one.
  • jingle bell rock is nearly unrecognizable to its original counterpart, but still has the spirit of the original composition.
  • christmas night was the first song made for the EP and is a personal favorite.
